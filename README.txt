Name of game : Into Darkness

Developed by : Grifin Butler

Narrative : A witch attempting to live her life is suddenly and accidentally taken into the world that powers her magic; one that she cannot survive in for too long. Trying to escape, she is assigned the creature that took her there in the first place. While being led out, the two come across a creature that poses a threat to both worlds. Can this threat be stopped, or will both worlds be forced to lose control of themselves?

Instructions : Start the game by typing in "source ./Start.sh"
